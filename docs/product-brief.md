# Product Brief — The Round Table

> Anchored to `discovery-notes.md`. PDF is canonical; founder voice is John Maciel's.

---

## 1. Vision

> **The Round Table is a private brotherhood for men who refuse to drift.** Five named men, seven pillars, one operating standard. No video-game talk. No vague encouragement. You bring intention, the table sharpens it — fitness, money, style, marriage, time, business, leadership. Men helping men, on the same journey, at a higher standard. The kind of room your grandfather would have respected.

(Voice calibration: John Maciel — direct, no fluff, grounded in the PDF's exact research. Avoid Tate-style intensity in landing copy; *that* lives in member-only spaces.)

## 2. Mission

Build a self-sustaining ecosystem where men prosper through **high-ROI relationships** with peers on the same journey and **direct training from named experts** in the seven domains the modern man neglects. (PDF p.3–4.)

1. Fitness
2. Investing
3. Style
4. Relationship Building
5. Time Management
6. Business Building
7. Leadership

All product surfaces map to one or more of these. Nothing ships that doesn't.

## 4. Personas

Anchored to the PDF's polling data (p.5) and the named founding circle.

### P1 — "The Drifter" (acquisition persona)
- 25–38, employed, income $60–150K, possibly married, no men he can rely on.
- Falls into the PDF's 80% with ≤2 trusted men, 20% who never discuss finances/goals, 60% who "often" lack purpose.
- Came in via Instagram, watched John for 6+ months, applied during a wave.
- Wants: a room of men who don't talk about video games. Standards. A reason to log in tomorrow.
- Tier: enters at **New Member ($20)**.

### P2 — "The Operator" (core member)
- 28–45, runs or owns something (business, deal flow, side venture), already disciplined, wants leverage on the pillars he's weak in.
- Recognizes John, Manny, Chase, Erik, or Sean from their work. Pays to be in the same room.
- Tier: **Knight ($30)** — full content + structured challenges.

### P3 — "The Inner Circle" (Council)
- 30–50, high-income, wants direct access — group calls, expert Q&A, named events.
- Treats the $50/mo like a club membership, not a content subscription.
- Tier: **Council ($50)** — top of the table, capped seats.

### P4 — "The Knight Aspirant" (engagement persona)
- Already a Knight or Council, climbing the rank ladder, completing challenges, earning recognition.
- Drives engagement metrics — daily check-ins, weekly accountability posts, expert calls.
- This is the persona the loop is designed for.

### P5 — "The Expert" (admin persona)
- The four named knights (John, Manny, Erik, Sean). *(Chase removed from v1 per founder.)*
- Posts content, hosts calls, answers questions, runs subsection rooms.
- v1: only John has full admin. Expert tooling lights up incrementally.

## 6. Engagement loops

### Daily
- **Morning Standard** — a single founder-authored prompt (90 seconds to read, one action). Same time daily.
- **Subsection feed** — members post check-ins on the pillars they're working.
- **Push** — one daily push, never two. Either Morning Standard OR a live event reminder, never both.

### Weekly
- **Round Table Live** — one live session per week, founding-knight rotation. v1: replay-only (asset). v1.1: live.
- **Weekly Standards Review** — Sunday self-assessment: ratings across the 7 pillars (1–10), private to the member, plotted over time.
- **Knight Challenges** — themed weeklong missions (e.g., "Money Week: no impulse spend, log every transaction"). Completion contributes to rank.

### Monthly
- **Rank movement** — the rank ladder advances based on a transparent point system (check-ins + challenges + peer recognition + standards reviews). One promotion event per month for visible members.
- **Council Roundtable** — Council-tier-only live group call.

### Quarterly
- **Crucible Challenge** — multi-pillar campaign (~30–45 days). Top finishers get permanent recognition + named-knight 1:1.
- **Cohort onboarding** — new applicants are processed in waves; new wave gets an explicit cohort identity.

## 7. Brotherhood mechanics (the standards layer)

Anchored to the PDF's "Brotherhood Standard" language and John's Personal Operating System (brain).

- **Standards** — every member declares 3–5 personal standards on signup, tied to the 7 pillars. Visible on profile. Editable, but every change is logged (the table sees who's actually moving).
- **Check-ins** — short structured posts ("Today I trained / didn't drink / closed a deal / spent emotionally"). Standardized so they aggregate.
- **Rank ladder** — Squire → Knight → Knight-Commander → Council (cosmetic tier separate from billing tier; you can be a paying Knight-tier subscriber at *Squire* rank if you haven't earned it).
- **Accountability pairs** — at signup, member is matched 1:1 with another member by overlapping pillar focus. Both get a private weekly check-in thread.
- **Recognition** — earned digital insignia tied to challenge completion + peer endorsements. Visible to the whole table. Cannot be bought.
- **Honor code** — short, signed at signup. No bashing women, no political tribalism, no get-rich-quick pitches, no medical/legal advice that isn't framed as opinion. Violations = warning → suspension → removal. The five founding knights enforce.

## 9. Anti-features (what we will NOT build)

- **Likes-only feeds with no accountability hook** — every social action ties to a standard or a check-in.
- **Public follower counts / vanity metrics** — internal recognition only.
- **External-link feed posts** — keep the table inside the table. Resources go through Library curation.
- **DMs in v1** — explicitly deferred to v1.1 per user intake. Members coordinate inside accountability-pair threads and subsection feeds.
- **Generic "wellness" or "self-care" framing** — voice is masculine-direct, not soft.
- **AI chatbots impersonating experts** — every expert reply is the named knight's actual voice. AI can summarize, tag, and route but never reply as a person.
- **Political / religious filter walls** — the honor code handles this; no platform-level partisan content.
- **Discriminatory listing copy** — App Store / Play Store listings describe a private members' community, not a men-only product (access mechanics handled inside the app via application + paid tier).
- **Crypto/stock signals service** — Manny + Sean teach frameworks, never publish trades as signals.

## 10. Voice & copy rules

- **Founder voice** (John): direct, tactical, masculine, no soft motivational language. Bio §3 + §16.
- **In-app strings**: terse, second-person, never apologetic. "Log your check-in" not "When you're ready, you can share an update."
- **Marketing copy**: leads with the PDF research stats (p.5). The numbers do the persuading.
- **Onboarding microcopy**: framed as oaths, not forms. ("State your three standards." not "Fill out your profile.")
- **No emojis in user-facing strings.** No exclamation points except in genuine recognition events.

## 11. Signup ritual & honor code (NEW USER FLOW)

New users do not see the Round Table feed until they have completed a four-step ritual. Non-skippable. Ships in M0.

### Step 1 — The brief (read-only screen)
Ivory type on charcoal. One screen. Founder voice.

> **The Round Table**
>
> This is a private brotherhood. Not a feed. Not a forum. Not a chat app.
>
> Most men have fewer than two people they can rely on. Most never talk about money or goals with their friends. We exist to change that — through standards, not slogans.
>
> Seven domains: fitness, investing, style, relationship building, time management, business building, leadership.
>
> Four men leading the rooms: John Maciel, Manny Thompson, Erik Sims, Sean Love.
>
> One operating premise: men helping men, on the same journey, at a higher standard.
>
> If that's not what you came for, the door is to your right.

CTA: **Continue.**

### Step 2 — The code (the honor code)
Bullet list of what the table is and is not. The "I agree" affordance is **disabled until the user scrolls to the bottom**.

**At the Round Table, we:**
- Show up. Check-ins, challenges, threads — we participate, we don't lurk.
- Speak directly. No empty motivation, no fake performance.
- Sharpen each other. Feedback is given honestly and received without ego.
- Hold confidences. What happens at the table stays at the table.
- Earn what we wear. Rank is earned, never bought.

**We do not:**
- Bash women, partners, or anyone outside the room.
- Pitch get-rich-quick schemes, MLMs, or affiliate funnels.
- Give medical, financial, or legal advice we wouldn't sign our name to.
- Tolerate political tribalism inside the rooms.
- Trade tier dues for influence — no one buys a louder voice.

Violation of the code routes to the founding knights. Action is at our discretion: warning, suspension, removal. No appeal.

CTA (enabled only after full-scroll): **I agree.**

> The agreement is timestamped: `profiles.honor_code_signed_at = now()`. Refusing routes to a goodbye screen and no payment is taken.

### Step 3 — The standards (declaration)
User declares 3–5 personal standards, each tied to one of the 7 pillars. Minimum one, maximum five. Examples shown, not pre-filled.

> "State your standards. These are visible on your profile. You can revise them — but every revision is logged. The table sees who's actually moving."

Schema: `profile_standards(pillar, statement)`. ≤140 chars per statement.

### Step 4 — The paywall
Tier selection (New Member / Knight / Council). Platform-appropriate purchase: Stripe on web; Apple IAP on iOS; Google Billing on Android. Optional 7-day trial on monthly.

After purchase: welcome screen announcing cohort (e.g., "Founding Knight, Class of 2026" for the first 50 Knight buyers), introducing the four founding knights, surfacing today's Daily Standard.

> **DECISION:** All four steps are mandatory and ordered. A user cannot reach Step 3 without `honor_code_signed_at` set. They cannot reach Step 4 without ≥1 standard saved. Culture before commerce.

## 12. DECISION summary

- **7 pillars**, not 8. PDF wins.
- **4 founding knights** named in v1 (John, Manny, Erik, Sean). Chase removed per founder.
- **5 subsection feeds + 1 primary feed + 1 Library.** IA is locked. Fitness = John solo.
- **3 billing tiers** (New Member / Knight / Council) **decoupled from rank ladder** (Squire / Knight / Knight-Commander / Council).
- **No DMs in v1.** Accountability pairs use scoped threads.
- **Daily push budget = 1.** Never two.
- **No Skool.** Owned platform from day one.
- **Founding Knights cohort** = first 50 paid members, rate-locked at $20 on Knight tier for 12 months, honoring the PDF's beta promise.
- **Signup ritual** = 4 mandatory steps: brief → code → standards → paywall.
